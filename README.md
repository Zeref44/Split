# 💸 Split

> *"Keep your friends close, and their half of the bill even closer."*

Split is a no-nonsense expense sharing app built to keep your friendships intact and your wallet accurate. No more *"I forgot my cash"* or *"Let's just split it 50/50"* when they ordered the steak.

---

## 😁 Hidden Easter Eggs (Long-Press Everything!)

I spent way too much time adding unnecessary long-press features. Go ahead, try them all! 

* **Split Icon** on the group list screen.
* **Group Cards** to reveal shortcuts.
* **Member Avatars**
* **Money Symbol** inside the add/edit expense screen.
* **Quick Amounts** under the money field.

---

## ⚡ Quick Gestures

* **Swipe Right/Left** expenses and history items
